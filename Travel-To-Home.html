Travel to Home!

Background Information

The coronavirus pandemic has caused thousands of deaths, lockdowns, and quarantines due to the virus' rapid spread. As such, travel restrictions and tourism are restricted; despite this, you can explore your world from home--digitally or physically. After all, some of nature's best offerings are right in your backyard and/or neighborhood!

What can you do at home?

Watch TV/movies

Meditate/exercise

Learn from various YouTube tutorials

Study

Play video games

How do you prevent the spread of coronavirus?

Wash. Your. Hands.

Exercise (increases immunity)

Eat a variety of foods (also increases immunity)

Wear a face covering when going outside (REALLY important!)

Self-isolate if you think you have the virus or test positive
